Refugee & Migrant Support Services

Providing guidance and assistance to help refugees and migrants settle safely, legally, and successfully in the UK

Our Support Services

At Nexus Tech International Ltd, we assist refugees, asylum seekers, and migrants with all aspects of settling in the UK. Our team provides guidance, support, and practical help across a wide range of services, including:

  • Help with applying for travel documents and official identification
  • Assistance with Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR) applications
  • Guidance and support for naturalization/citizenship applications
  • Support with Universal Credit and other welfare benefits applications
  • Assistance with finding employment and career guidance
  • Help with housing, accommodation, and relocation planning
  • Access to education, training, and skills development opportunities
  • Legal and welfare support through UK charities and government services
  • Advice on any refugee-related forms, challenges, or official applications

Why Choose Nexus Tech International Ltd?

  • Experienced in supporting refugees, asylum seekers, and migrants
  • Safe, confidential, and GDPR-compliant processes
  • Partnerships with UK charities, local authorities, and legal advisors
  • Personalized support tailored to each individual or family
  • Clear guidance through complex UK systems and paperwork
  • Focus on empowering clients to live independently and confidently
