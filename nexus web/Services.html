Refugee & Migrant Support

Providing guidance and assistance to help refugees and migrants settle safely and successfully in the UK.

Our Support Services

  • Help with travel documents and official ID
  • Assistance with Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR) applications
  • Guidance for naturalization/citizenship applications
  • Support with welfare benefits, including Universal Credit
  • Employment assistance and career guidance
  • Housing, accommodation, and relocation planning
  • Access to education, training, and skills development
  • Legal and non-legal welfare support through referral services
  • Advice on refugee-related forms, challenges, or official applications

  • Clear guidance through complex UK systems
  • Empowering clients to live independently and confidently
