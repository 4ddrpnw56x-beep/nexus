Refugee & Migrant Support Services

Practical, non-legal guidance and support to help refugees, asylum seekers, and migrants settle safely, confidently, and independently in the UK.

Our Support Services

At Nexus Tech International Ltd, we assist refugees, asylum seekers, and migrants with all aspects of settling in the UK. Our team provides guidance, support, and practical help across a wide range of services, including:

  • Help with applying for travel documents and official identification
  • Assistance with Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR) applications
  • Guidance and support for naturalisation/citizenship applications
  • Support with Universal Credit and other welfare benefits applications
  • Assistance with finding employment and career guidance
  • Help with housing, accommodation, and relocation planning
  • Access to education, training, and skills development opportunities
  • Referrals to trusted UK charities, local authorities, and support services
  • Advice on any refugee-related forms, challenges, or official applications
Important Notice:
Nexus Tech International Ltd does not provide legal advice, immigration advice, or representation. We offer non-legal guidance, practical support, and referrals only. Where legal advice or regulated services are required, clients are referred to qualified and authorised professionals.

Common Challenges Refugees & Migrants Face

  • Understanding UK systems, processes, and paperwork
  • Language and communication barriers
  • Accessing housing and suitable accommodation
  • Applying for welfare benefits and financial support
  • Finding employment and understanding UK workplace culture
  • CV writing, job applications, and interview preparation
  • Accessing education, training, and skills development
  • Digital exclusion and lack of access to online services
  • Social isolation and lack of community support
  • Understanding healthcare registration and GP access
  • Relocation challenges within the UK
  • Stress related to adjustment, uncertainty, and integration
