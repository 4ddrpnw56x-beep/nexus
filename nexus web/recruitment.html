We connect highly skilled workers with employers in the UK and support successful relocation from around the world.

Our Recruitment Mission

At Nexus Tech International Ltd, our mission is to bridge talent and opportunity by sourcing, screening, and placing skilled professionals into UK organisations across sectors. We build long‑term workforce solutions based on deep industry knowledge and strong global talent networks.

Strategic Global Partnership

We are proudly partnered with Tofam Foreign Employment Agency in Ethiopia, enabling us to source and recruit qualified workers from Ethiopia and expand our reach to other talent markets worldwide. This agreement strengthens our ability to facilitate ethical, transparent, and high‑quality international placements.

What We Offer Employers

  • Global Talent Sourcing: Access to highly skilled candidates from Ethiopia, Africa, and across the world.
  • Candidate Screening & Selection: Comprehensive pre‑screening, interviews, and skill assessment to match employer criteria.
  • Visa & Immigration Support Guidance: Practical support with documentation and understanding UK immigration pathways.
  • Compliance & Regulatory Assistance: Assistance with right‑to‑work checks and UK employment compliance.
  • Relocation Coordination: Helping candidates transition smoothly with travel, accommodation, and settling‑in support.
  • Custom Workforce Solutions: Tailored recruitment solutions for permanent, contract, and project‑based roles.

What We Offer Candidates

  • Profile Evaluation & Career Guidance: Professional evaluation of skills and career pathways in the UK job market.
  • Interview Preparation & Skill Matching: Preparation support to help you succeed in employer interviews.
  • Relocation Support: Practical guidance on travel, accommodation, and settling into your new destination.
  • Visa Documentation Assistance: Clear explanations of required documentation and referral to regulated advisors where needed.
  • Integration Support: Advice on local services, community orientation, and cultural adaptation.
Non‑Legal Reminder: Our recruitment guidance is intended for practical support. We do not provide immigration or legal representation. For legal advice, please consult a qualified legal professional.
