Our Story

Nexus Tech International Ltd was founded in 2024 in the UK with a vision to provide integrated solutions for tech, travel, recruitment, and refugee/migrant support. Over the years, we have expanded our services to meet the evolving needs of our clients while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and trust.
